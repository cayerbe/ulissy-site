The Problem

Identity on the internet is broken.

Today's Reality
$56B lost to identity fraud annually

Every system online verifies accounts, not humans. Bots create millions of fake accounts. Passwords get stolen. KYC costs $5-30 per verification. And after all that — you still can't prove who's behind the screen.

  • Passwords stolen in 81% of breaches
  • KYC takes 3-5 days and costs $5-30 per user
  • Biometrics require special hardware
  • Bots pass CAPTCHA 98.9% of the time
GNS Approach
Prove identity through movement

A bot can fake a password. It can solve a CAPTCHA. It can even generate a face. But it cannot fake months of real-world physical movement across diverse locations with consistent human patterns.

  • One Ed25519 key = identity + wallet + encryption
  • Verification in milliseconds, costs fractions of a cent
  • Works on any smartphone, no special hardware
  • Privacy by math: H3 cells, not raw GPS

The Process

How trajectory identity works.

Four steps from anonymous device to verified identity. No forms, no uploads, no waiting.

Generate Keys
An Ed25519 keypair is created on your device. This single key becomes your identity, your message signing key, your encryption key, and your payment wallet.
Collect Breadcrumbs
Your device emits cryptographically-signed location breadcrumbs, quantized to H3 hexagonal cells. Each links to the previous, forming an unforgeable chain.
Build Trust
The Trajectory Criticality Engine scores your movement across three axes: entropy, regularity, and span. At 100 breadcrumbs + trust score 20, claim your @handle.
Prove Anything
Use your trajectory-verified identity to authenticate, sign messages, send payments, and prove you're human — all with one key, zero passwords.
