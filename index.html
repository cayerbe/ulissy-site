Protocol

How Proof-of-Trajectory works

1

Move through the world

Your phone collects location breadcrumbs as you go about your day. No special hardware — any smartphone works.

2

Privacy-preserving quantization

Raw GPS is never stored or transmitted. Locations are quantized to H3 hexagonal cells (~15,000 m²) — enough to prove movement, not enough to track you.

3

Sign and chain

Each breadcrumb is signed with your Ed25519 key and hash-linked to the previous one, forming an unforgeable chain of presence.

4

Bundle into Epochs

100+ breadcrumbs form an Epoch — a verifiable proof that a real human moved through real space over real time.

5

Earn trust, claim your @handle

Epochs accumulate into a trust score. At threshold, claim your permanent @handle. No email, no phone number. Just your trajectory.
